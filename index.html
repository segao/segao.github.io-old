My name is Sharon Gao and I am a 20 year old student currently entering my third year of a four-year Computer Science B.S. program at the University of Pittsburgh main campus. I was formerly a pre-med student majoring in Chemistry--that is until I took my first programming class to satisfy an elective requirement. By the end of the semester, I knew I found my true calling and had to make the switch.

I love getting my hands dirty and getting the "aha!" moment following a long, drawn-out debugging process. I'm always looking for more opportunities to learn and gain experience!

In addition to my interests in programming and coding, I am very passionate in web design, video games, volunteering, exercising, cooking, and hiking. After graduation, I hope to gain experience in the work force as a web developer before returning to school to study Computer Science at a graduate level.
